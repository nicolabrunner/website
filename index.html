Hello!

My name is Nicola Brunner, I'm 20 years old and I live in Riva San Vitale, near Mendrisio (Switzerland of course).

Why Informatics?

I have chosen to study informatics, because it's something I am very interested in and where I see great opportunities for the future. In fact, my first idea was to study or to work with something related to the medias: photographer, cameraman, audio technician - but I figured out that it's very difficult to find a job in this environment and so I decided to go for the informatic (but with the idea to utilize informatic for something related to the medias… finding a way to link them).

And before?

Before the university of Lugano, I have studied chemistry and biology at the Liceo di Mendrisio, I wasn't very good at the main subjects, but I compensated the bad notes with good notes that I obtained in other subjects, for example languages.

Languages?

I speak many languages - slightly exaggerated - but I speak very well Italian and German (also Swiss-German) and quite well French and English.

Experiences?

This year between my last school and the university I took a break and utilized the time for doing two Internships and for doing my obligatory time in the Swiss Army. I worked for a press agency based in Ticino for some months, as well as for an informatic company based in Zurich. Since January I serve at the Swiss Armed Forces, in the Rescue Troops as driver and in the last months I also had the possibility to promote as sergeant.

Hobbies?

As you can see in above presentation my hobbies are photography, videomaking and of course sport. I go often running and in wintertime I practices a lot of skiing.
